Who we are

Engineering precision, meet accounting depth.

EdgeCostSeg is the cost segregation practice of Real Deal Accounting — combining engineering rigor with hands-on tax expertise so investors capture every dollar they're entitled to, defensibly.

Our approach

Built for owners, in partnership with their advisors.

We started EdgeCostSeg because too many real estate owners were leaving money on the table — not from a lack of effort, but from a lack of specialized analysis.

Backed by Real Deal Accounting, we bring the engineering discipline a defensible study requires and the accounting fluency to make sure the results actually land on your return. We don't replace your CPA — we make their job easier and your outcome stronger.
