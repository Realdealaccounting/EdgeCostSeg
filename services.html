What we do

Depreciation strategy, engineered end to end.

From your first study to ongoing portfolio support, every service is built on the same engineering rigor — and designed to work seamlessly with your CPA.

Engineering-based cost segregation

Our core study. We analyze plans, costs, and on-site components to reclassify assets into 5-, 7-, and 15-year schedules — delivering an accurate, audit-ready report.

How studies work

Look-back studies

Bought years ago? A look-back study captures depreciation you never claimed and brings it into the current year — generally without amending prior returns.

See if you qualify

Purchase price allocation

For acquisitions, we help allocate the purchase price across land, building, and components — establishing a clean, defensible basis from day one.

Talk it through

Partial asset disposition

Renovating or replacing components? We help quantify the remaining basis of what's removed so you can write it off — instead of depreciating a ghost.
