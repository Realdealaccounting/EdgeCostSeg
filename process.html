The process

How we reduce your tax burden.

There's nothing for you to do — we handle the engagement from start to finish.

1

No-cost feasibility analysis

We confirm your property is a strong candidate and model the likely benefit against the study cost — at no charge, with no obligation to proceed.

Timeline: a day or two  ·  Cost: free

2

Property tour & engineering review

One of our engineers reviews the property and identifies every asset that can be reclassified into a shorter 5-, 7-, or 15-year schedule.

We handle: classification & cost allocation

3

Audit-ready report

We compile a detailed, defensible cost segregation report — the engineering methodology plus a CPA-ready summary your accountant can apply directly.

You receive: full study + CPA summary

4

Applied to your tax return

Your CPA plugs the figures straight into your return's depreciation schedule — so you keep more of your money this year, not decades from now.

The result: bigger deductions, sooner
